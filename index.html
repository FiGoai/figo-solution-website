How FiGo Works with Google Cloud

AI-Powered Matching

Our proprietary algorithm runs on Google Cloud's Vertex AI, analyzing user preferences, trainer expertise, and scheduling patterns to create perfect matches.

Location Services

Google Maps Platform integration allows users to find trainers and sessions nearby, with precise mapping of gyms, parks, and other training locations.

Real-Time Platform

Firebase provides real-time chat, booking confirmations, push notifications, and live session updates for a seamless user experience.

Scalable Infrastructure

Google Kubernetes Engine ensures high availability and automatic scaling during peak booking times across our global user base.

Data-Driven Insights

BigQuery analyzes session trends and user behavior to optimize our matching models and business strategies for both B2C and B2B segments.

Dual Marketplace

Platform serves individual consumers (B2C) and corporate partners (B2B) including companies, wellness programs, gyms, and hospitality providers.
